For Delegators

Why stake with The Expanse?

  • Conservative upgrades to avoid unnecessary instability
  • Dedicated monitoring and alerting pipelines
  • US-based operator with identifiable accountability
  • Strong observability (logs, metrics, backups)
  • Operational patterns reused and refined across both Sentinel and Akash for consistency
Risk Philosophy

Operational discipline

We prioritize uptime and predictable operations over chasing the newest features. All upgrades and network changes are rehearsed and staged, with a focus on rollback strategies and clear communication.

How to stake

Delegating to The Expanse on Sentinel

Step 1

Locate the validator

In your Sentinel-compatible wallet, search for the moniker "The Expanse | Infinite Possibilities".

Step 2

Verify details

Confirm the commission rate (5%), identity, and any details listed on this page before delegating.

Step 3

Delegate & monitor

Delegate through your wallet and monitor rewards over time. You can use the Wallet Tracker to explore delegation distribution.
