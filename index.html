About

What is Orbital Forge?

Orbital Forge, LLC is a blockchain infrastructure and tooling company focused on secure, transparent, and resilient validator operations. We build and maintain infrastructure so delegators can stake with confidence, and we contribute back to the ecosystem through open tooling and Docker-based deployments.

Validator

The Expanse – Sentinel Network Validator

The Expanse is a validator on the Sentinel blockchain, operated by Orbital Forge, LLC. Our goal is to provide reliable uptime, careful monitoring, and defensive security practices while remaining an active participant in the community.

  • Operated by a US-based, registered LLC (Orbital Forge, LLC)
  • Focus on security, observability, and fail-safe operations
  • Actively maintained Docker-based infrastructure for Sentinel

If you’re a delegator, you can support the network by staking with The Expanse. (Validator address and explorer links will go here.)
