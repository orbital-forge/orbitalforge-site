The company

Focused, independent photonics development.

Orbital Forge conducts independently developed research in integrated photonics, RF-to-optical signal conversion, optical information processing, calibration, and ruggedized photonic packaging.

The company is structured to mature technical concepts through modeling, foundry fabrication, packaging, test, and measured validation while protecting detailed technical information through controlled partner discussions.

Current development focus

Receive-Chain Test Vehicle.

A foundry-accessible development vehicle for evaluating the essential elements of an integrated RF-photonic receive chain.

The test vehicle is intended to support measured evaluation of:

  • RF-to-optical conversion
  • Low-loss photonic routing and processing
  • Signal and reference optical paths
  • Monitoring, bias, and thermal control
  • Balanced detection
  • Environmental stability

Detailed architecture, performance objectives, diagrams, and technical roadmaps are available only through controlled discussions with qualified partners.
