Our thesis

Better sensing can begin with a different architecture.

Performance does not always come from adding more power, aperture, or processing. It can come from changing how signals are acquired, combined, processed, and interpreted.

Orbital Forge explores those architectural opportunities through focused analysis, rapid prototyping, and evidence-led technical evaluation.
