Why it matters

Built for evidence, not slideware.

Early R&D often fails because the concept is never forced through real interfaces, real data assumptions, operational constraints, or measurable tests. Orbital Forge focuses on proving the thin slice first.

  • Builder-led execution. Strategy and architecture stay tied to working code and demos.
  • Short feedback loops. Prototype early, test assumptions, then decide whether to scale or stop.
  • Operational realism. Interfaces, logs, replay, metrics, and transition constraints are first-class.
  • Government-aware posture. Designed for bounded milestones, auditability, and responsible AI adoption.

Have a mission problem that needs a prototype?

Orbital Forge can help shape the concept, build the first working slice, and package the evidence needed for the next decision.
