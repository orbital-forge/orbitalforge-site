Risk reduction

Built for milestone-based prototype work.

Government R&D succeeds when the work is bounded, testable, and honest about risk. Orbital Forge structures prototype efforts around visible evidence gates.

Gate 1

Problem thread

Define the mission thread, actors, data sources, and success measures.

Gate 2

Working prototype

Demonstrate the critical path with real interfaces or realistic surrogates.

Gate 3

Evaluation evidence

Use logs, replay, operator review, and metrics to show what is repeatable.

Gate 4

Transition decision

Produce a roadmap, dependencies, risks, and path to production hardening.

Responsible AI posture

  • Human review for high-consequence recommendations
  • Traceable sources, prompts, outputs, and decision evidence
  • Configuration-controlled models and workflows
  • Clear distinction between automation, recommendation, and approved tasking
  • Security, provenance, and access-control considerations from the start
